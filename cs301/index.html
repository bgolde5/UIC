Instructions:

The alphabet of the language consists of:

{ ‘a’, ‘b’, ‘c’, ‘d’, ‘|’, ‘*’, ‘(‘, ‘)’ }.

The following are valid regular expressions:

  • a
  • abcd
  • a|b
  • ab|bc|c|d
  • (a|bc)*
  • abc*
  • abc***
  • a*bc*d
  • a*|b*
  • a|b|c|d(a|b)c*
  • (((a)*)|((b)*))b
  • (a)(b)(c)|(d)*

The following are invalid regular expressions:

  • a|
  • a||b
  • |a
  • ()
  • a()
  • *
  • *|
  • |*
